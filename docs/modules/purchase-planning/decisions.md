## 2026-04-30 - Saved plans at chain + department grain

**Context:** The v1 calculator was too category-specific and did not persist planning work. Buyers need a saved seasonal plan at chain + department grain, while PO creation stays in Purchasing.

**Decision:** Purchase Planning V2 saves three-month seasonal plans in `app.purchase_plan*` tables keyed by store group, season, season year, and selected departments. Holt-Winters is the default forecast. Discount-heavy historical months are down-weighted before forecasting. Buy math subtracts on-hand, current/future on-order, and native committed open PO residuals. Adjustments are department season-total changes only: signed percent lift/reduction or absolute total override, both with required reason and audit trail.

**Consequences:** The UI is now saved-plan-first instead of ad-hoc calculator-first. The main grid presents department season totals first and expands into month detail. Baseline calculated rows and current adjusted rows are stored side by side for comparison. No purchase orders, vendor picks, SKU picks, or draft worksheets are generated by this module.

**Alternatives considered:** Category grain was rejected as too specific for the buyer workflow. PO generation was rejected for this slice to keep Purchasing as the PO authority. Per-month/manual line edits were deferred in favor of auditable department season-level adjustments.
